I'm Sreedath Panat, a Ph.D. from MIT (2017-2022) with roots in Mechanical Engineering at IIT Madras (2012-2017). I graduated as the topper of my department at IITM. My MIT research advised by Prof. Kripa Varanasi focused on charged interfaces and energy sustainability, leading to notable publications and collaborations. Beyond academia, I've co-founded ed-tech ventures Vizuara and Videsh with my IITM classmates, who are also alumni of MIT and Purdue University. I have also been a part of an MIT spin-off AgZen winning more that $600k in business pitch competitions for AgZen. I also initiated a startup "Caanvi" during the pandemic for mentoring Indian students. My work has earned awards like the deFlorez competition and been featured in MIT's Spotlight. I've held positions at MIT's biggest cultural organization Sangam and been a Chief Speaker at international webinars. Explore my work and join my innovation journey.
